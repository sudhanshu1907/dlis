Podcast

The Department of Library and Information Science (DLIS) has launched an innovative initiative using Google NotebookLM AI to transform academic articles into interactive podcasts. Unlike traditional e-books, this AI-powered solution enhances accessibility and engagement by converting text into dynamic, conversational audio content. Listeners can interact with the material and receive AI-generated explanations, making learning more immersive. This initiative not only modernizes information dissemination but also caters to diverse learning preferences, ensuring a richer, more engaging educational experience for students and researchers.
