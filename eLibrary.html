DU e-Library

Delhi University's e-Library, managed by the Delhi University Library System (DULS), offers students, faculty, and staff extensive access to a wide array of electronic resources. This includes e-journals, e-books, and databases covering various disciplines. The e-Library serves as a comprehensive platform, enabling users to access millions of subscribed and open-access resources anytime and from anywhere.

To facilitate effective use of these resources, DULS provides tutorials and guidance on searching and navigating the e-Library. For instance, the "DU eLibrary | Tutorial 3 | Searching Effectively" video offers insights into optimizing search strategies within the platform.

Additionally, DULS maintains an active presence on social media platforms like Facebook, where they share updates, tips, and information about new resources and services available through the e-Library.

For mobile users, the DU eLibrary app provides convenient access to the university's electronic resources on Android devices, ensuring that users can engage with academic materials on-the-go.

Overall, Delhi University's e-Library stands as a vital academic resource, supporting the university community's research and learning endeavors through its extensive digital collections and user-friendly platforms.
